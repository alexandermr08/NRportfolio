MARKETING CALENDARS

T3 | The Think Tank

After over 25 years of relying on relationship building and strong client referrals, T3 decided the best course of action was to build an in-house marketing department to support their own new business initiatives and secure new clients.

T3 hired Casey Gannon as Marketing Director, and I became her direct support. I created multiple marketing calendars where all content was centrally located to better monitor all projects and track initiatives.

Beyond project management and tracking, my duties included working with the various agency departments to develop content for award submissions, event collateral, and any additional marketing needs.
