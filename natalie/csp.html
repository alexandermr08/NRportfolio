T3 | The Think Tank

T3, a full-service advertising agency, is headquartered in Austin with offices in New York and San Francisco. The firm emphasized new business efforts and needed a solution to create strong content quickly.

However, there was no structure in place to quantify campaign results for the business development team.

In my role of assisting executive leadership's marketing efforts, I successfully developed and implemented an agency-wide procedure which would capture campaign results to provide critical information to leadership. To accomplish this, interviews were held with key project leads and department heads to ensure the new procedure would work for the entire agency.
